Psychometric Scales Repository

Psychologists regularly use psychometric scales to collect data about peoples attitudes, personality, perceptions, or behaviour (amongst other things).

The questionnaires, scales, and inventories listed below are a great place to get started on looking for a suitable measure for your research. Although, there are many measures that are listed below. You may find a more suitable measure by conducting a Library Search, using Google Scholar, or by other means. If you come across something interesting that you think we should look at, please let us know!

—————————DISCLAIMER——————————

We do not have copyright authority over some measures and cannot grant permission for use.

We recommend students from Wrexham University to use the “Free for use” scales. If you require a particular scale, then please contact the author(s) of the questionnaire for use permissions. However, please consult your supervisor before proceeding.

The scales are provided solely as a courtesy to help Dissertation students develop their experiments efficiently, to get their projects underway, and provide module leaders resources for their teaching modules. Not all scales are provided and will only be available when Wrexham students, researchers and module leaders provide the appropriate evidence of licensing or permission.

Please note that the scales may contain errors. If you identify any inaccuracies, kindly contact psychology.technician@wrexham.ac.uk. While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, these materials are prepared by humans and should be carefully checked before use in any research or teaching capacity.

“Additionally, it is the responsibility of each researcher to ensure the information they use from this repository is accurate and complies with copyright law. For more information about copyright and how it applies to research materials, please see the UK Government’s guidance on copyright: https://www.gov.uk/copyright

Please note: Due to the volume of requests, emails requesting permission for use will not receive a reply. We are also unable to provide author contact information.

If you have further questions, you can contact: psychology.technician@wrexham.ac.uk

This page organises validated questionnaires into key psychological domains. You can access the Gorilla version of these tasks here: Wrexham PsychTech Open Materials
